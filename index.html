Explore Thirukoilur, Thiruvannamalai & Villupuram

Discover the heritage, culture, and scenic beauty of these remarkable regions of Tamil Nadu.

Heritage of the Region

Tirukoilur, also spelt as Tirukkoyilur or Tirukovilur, is a historic town located on the southern bank of the Thenpennai River. It is home to the Ulagalantha Perumal Temple with one of the tallest gopurams in Tamil Nadu, as well as Veerateeswarar Temple and Kabilar Kundru, which are of religious and historical significance. The region’s ancient past is enriched by its association with Tamil poets and royal figures.

Thiruvannamalai is a sacred town nestled near Arunachala Hill, where pilgrims perform the Girivalam circumambulation as an act of devotion. The Arunachaleswarar Temple is a grand architectural marvel dedicated to Lord Shiva. The town is also associated with spiritual luminaries such as Sri Seshadri Swamigal and Sri Ramana Maharshi.

Villupuram is a vibrant cultural hub with historical landmarks like Gingee Fort, known as the 'Troy of the East', and serene pilgrimage sites like Parvathamalai. Its markets reflect traditional arts, crafts, and local cuisine, offering a glimpse into the living culture of rural Tamil Nadu.

Important Places Around the Area

Arunachaleswarar Temple

A massive and ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, situated at the base of Arunachala Hill. It is a focal point for spiritual seekers and houses intricate stone carvings and sacred rituals performed daily.

View Map

Parvathamalai

A mystical mountain filled with spiritual energy, believed to be the abode of divine forces. The temple at the peak remains open throughout the year, welcoming pilgrims and trekkers.

View Map

Gingee Fort

Built across centuries by various dynasties, this fort stands as a symbol of military architecture. Visitors can explore watchtowers, gateways, and remnants of the past while enjoying panoramic views.

View Map

Sathanur Dam

A significant irrigation structure built over the Thenpennai River, surrounded by lush landscapes. It provides water to nearby farmlands and is a serene spot for nature lovers and picnickers.

View Map

Kabilar Kundru

A rock formation in the river associated with the Tamil poet Kapilar. This site stands as a testament to Tamil literary tradition and spiritual dedication, offering tranquil surroundings for visitors.

View Map

Thenpennai River

The lifeline of the region, this river nourishes agriculture and sustains local communities. Visitors can enjoy riverside walks and experience rural life close to nature.

View Map

Ulagalantha Perumal Temple

A revered Vishnu temple known for its towering gopuram and rich mythological traditions. It played a pivotal role in the Vaishnavism movement and continues to attract devotees and scholars alike.

View Map

Veerateeswarar Temple

A sacred Shiva temple where devotees perform rituals to seek blessings and spiritual growth. The temple’s architecture and ambiance reflect centuries of devotion and cultural richness.
